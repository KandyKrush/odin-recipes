Paneer Tikka Masala!

Paneer Tikka Masala is a staple Indian dish, made from cubes of paneer cheese in a spicy creamy curry sauce. It tastes super good!

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup of butter
  • 1 pound of paneer, cut into 1/2 inch cubes
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 medium jalapeno peppers, chopped
  • 1 tbsp ground cashews
  • 1 tsp ginger paste
  • 1 tsp garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 can of tomato paste
  • 1 tsp salt
